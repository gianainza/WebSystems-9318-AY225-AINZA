About Bacoor City

The City of Bacoor

Bacoor, officially the City of Bacoor, is a 1st class component city in the province of Cavite, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 664,625 people, making it the most populous city in Cavite.

Bacoor is part of the Greater Manila Area, located approximately 15 kilometers southwest of Manila. It was officially converted into a city in 2012 after a plebiscite ratified Republic Act No. 10160.

The city is known for its historical significance, being one of the sites of the Battle of Zapote Bridge during the Philippine Revolution. Today, Bacoor is a rapidly urbanizing city with a thriving economy and vibrant community.

Vision
