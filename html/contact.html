Frequently Asked Questions

Business permits can be obtained from the City Treasurer's Office. You need to submit required documents including Barangay Clearance, DTI/SEC Registration, and proof of business address. The process typically takes 3-5 working days.

Birth certificates can be obtained from the City Civil Registry Office located at the City Hall. You need to present valid ID and pay the required fee. For births registered in Bacoor, processing is same day.

You can report barangay concerns through your barangay hall, or use the contact form on this website selecting "Complaint" as the subject. For urgent matters, contact the City Mayor's Office hotline at (046) 434-9801.
